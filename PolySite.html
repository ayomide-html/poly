We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of plastic bottles, including PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), and PC (Polycarbonate) bottles. Our products cater to various industries, including beverages, food, pharmaceuticals, personal care, and household products.

Yes, we ship our products globally. We work with reliable logistics partners to ensure your orders are delivered safely and on time, no matter where you are located.
